# Global_Condom_Usage_Dataset
Data analysis project exploring global condom usage, teen pregnancy rates, and HIV awareness to evaluate the impact of education and government campaigns.
# 📊 Global Condom Usage Analysis

### Exploratory Data Analysis Project

## 📁 Project Overview

This project focuses on **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** of a global dataset related to **condom usage, teen pregnancy rates, and HIV awareness**. The goal is to uncover patterns, trends, and relationships that can provide meaningful insights into public health awareness and intervention effectiveness.

---

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Most Popular Condom Type

A **bar chart** was created to identify the most commonly used condom types based on sales data.
This helps in understanding **consumer preferences** and market demand.

---

### 2. Teen Pregnancy Rate Trend (2015–2025)

A **line plot** was used to analyze changes in teen pregnancy rates over time.

* Data was grouped by year
* Average pregnancy rate was calculated

This analysis helps evaluate whether **awareness and intervention programs** have improved outcomes over the decade.

---

### 3. Country-wise Comparison

A **grouped bar chart** was used to compare:

* Teen Pregnancy Rate
* HIV Prevention Awareness (%)

This comparison highlights:

* Countries with strong awareness programs
* Regions needing improved education and outreach

---

### 4. Government Campaign Effectiveness

* Converted **Government Campaigns (Yes/No)** into binary values (1/0)
* Calculated **Pearson correlation** with HIV Awareness (%)
* Visualized results using a **boxplot**

This analysis helps determine the **impact of government initiatives** on public awareness.

---

## Key Insights

 **Declining or rising trends** in teen pregnancy rates indicate the effectiveness of awareness programs
* Significant **variation across countries** in both awareness and outcomes
**Government campaigns** show a positive correlation with higher HIV prevention awareness
